+ VISUAL IDENTITY

GUIMARÃES BEER FEST

1ª EDIÇÃO

For Guimarães Beer Fest, I led the creative process from concept to final execution, developing a bold and contemporary brand identity. The visual language centers on three core experiences: Sounds (music and live shows), Flavors (craft beer culture), and Friendship (shared moments and celebration).

The branding is energetic and approachable, with playful yellow bubbles that evoke both the effervescence of beer and the lively, festive spirit of the event. Every design element—from posters to digital assets and event materials—was crafted to create immediate impact and ensure strong visibility in urban and digital environments.

The typography is dynamic and friendly, while the color palette is vibrant and inviting, reflecting the joyful atmosphere of the festival. Messaging and visuals work together to communicate a journey through taste, sound, and connection. Whether in print, on social media, or at the event site, the brand invites the audience to discover new flavors, enjoy live performances, and create lasting memories with friends.

By combining bold graphic design with an authentic sense of community, the Guimarães Beer Fest branding succeeds in positioning the event as a highlight in the city—an unmissable gathering that celebrates music, craft beer, and the pleasure of being together.


GUIMARÃES BEER FEST

2ª EDIÇÃO

For the second edition of Guimarães Beer Fest, my goal was to create a strong sense of continuity while introducing a fresh and joyful visual energy. Building on the iconic look and feel established in the first year, I maintained the bold typography and vibrant yellow palette—key elements that had already become closely associated with the festival’s identity.

To evolve the brand for this new edition, I introduced playful, balloon-inspired lettering and design details, enhancing the sense of fun and celebration. The “Summer Edition” visual language is bright, dynamic, and unmistakably festive, capturing the lively spirit of open-air music, craft beer tastings, and social gatherings.

Every element, from large-scale posters to social media graphics and on-site materials, was carefully crafted to ensure both recognition and novelty. The consistent branding guarantees immediate association with the festival, while the new design touches communicate that this is a unique, special edition—one that brings together old friends and new faces in a joyful, welcoming environment.

The result is a visual identity that feels both familiar and exciting, encouraging audiences to return year after year for new experiences, new flavors, and lasting memories—all in the heart of Guimarães.

SOPÉ

KITCHEN & BAR

SOPÉ Kitchen & Bar is a contemporary fine dining restaurant where culinary artistry meets sophisticated ambiance. The brand identity was meticulously crafted to evoke both luxury and modernity, appealing to discerning guests seeking an elevated dining experience.

The name SOPÉ, meaning “foothill” in Portuguese, was chosen to reflect the restaurant’s unique location—at the base of Guimarães’ most iconic mountain, and in the building that marks the start of the city’s famous cable car journey. This connection to place inspired a refined color palette of earthy beiges and terracotta tones, subtly referencing the natural surroundings and heritage of Guimarães.

The elegant, custom typography blends classic serifs with contemporary design, striking a balance between timeless sophistication and current trends. All brand applications, from menus and digital assets to staff uniforms, feature understated design details that enhance the perception of exclusivity. The brand’s imagery highlights carefully curated dishes and artisanal cocktails, inviting guests to savor each moment in an environment of relaxed luxury.

Overall, the SOPÉ brand identity successfully communicates a sense of place and purpose, inviting guests to experience the culinary delights of Guimarães in a setting that feels both welcoming and refined. It’s a brand that stands out in the competitive fine dining landscape, promising an unforgettable journey through taste and culture.


BOUTCHE

VERMUTERIA & CERVEJARIA

Boutche is more than just a vermouth bar – it’s a playful escape in the heart of Guimarães where every visit promises good drinks and even better moments. The goal behind this branding was to combine a lighthearted, friendly spirit with a visually bold and professional identity.

The visual universe of Boutche stands out through its quirky custom typography, dynamic color palette, and illustrated elements that bring character and warmth to every touchpoint, from the menus to merchandise. The branding is rooted in the idea of gathering friends and celebrating the small pleasures of life, always with a glass of vermouth in hand.

The entire visual system was designed to be instantly recognizable and approachable: playful illustrations, witty copywriting, and vibrant colors create a welcoming environment where everyone feels at home. Each detail—from the hand-drawn motifs to the casual copywriting—invites guests to relax, laugh, and savor authentic moments together.

Boutche isn’t just about drinks—it’s about connection, community, and celebrating life with a smile. Whether you’re picking up a Negroni, a beer, or posting a photo with one of the most beautiful streets of Guimarães, you become part of a story where good vermouth and good beer go hand in hand.


+ PACKAGING

DESLIZE

CLIENTE: QUINTA DO EIDINHO

For the Deslize wine brand, the goal was to create packaging that pays homage to classic wine traditions while embracing a fresh, contemporary twist. The label design stands out with its clean lines, elegant typography, and a distinctive diagonal cut—a visual metaphor for the brand name “Deslize” (meaning “slide” in Portuguese). This subtle detail not only adds a dynamic sense of movement, but also sets the brand apart from conventional wine labels, reflecting a spirit of innovation grounded in heritage.

Rooted in the picturesque Douro Valley, specifically in the Baixo Corgo region, Deslize wines celebrate the richness and authenticity of Portuguese winemaking. Each bottle carries the essence of the Douro, combining time-honored craftsmanship with a modern visual identity. The color palette and material choices were carefully selected to convey sophistication and exclusivity, while the distinctive label cut invites curiosity and interaction.

Deslize is a brand for wine lovers who appreciate both tradition and a touch of originality—where every bottle is a seamless slide between past and present, classic and contemporary.
